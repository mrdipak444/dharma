About

Dharmachowk Marbles And Tiles is Chitwan's biggest Marbles and Tiles wholesale supplier and retailer for commercial and residential use. We are Nepal’s most preferred brand for extra premium quality natural marbles and Tiles. Our products are imported from all around the world and over the years.

We are one of the largest marbles, stones, granites, marble handicrafts, and tiles adhesives suppliers in Chitwan. For over 20 years we have been transmitting the value and the original beauty of Natural Stone. We have a wide range of products to serve the varied requirements of our customers.
